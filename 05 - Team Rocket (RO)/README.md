# Team Rocket (RO)

The fourth Pokemon TCG expansion set, released on April 24, 2000. Team Rocket introduced 83 cards featuring "Dark" Pokemon - corrupted versions trained by the villainous Team Rocket organization. This set brought a darker theme and unique mechanics to the game.

## Notable Cards

- **Dark Charizard** (#004/021) - Dark Fire Spin deals massive damage
- **Dark Blastoise** (#003/020) - Rocket Tackle provides recoil-based aggression
- **Dark Vileplume** (#013/030) - Hay Fever blocks Trainer cards from both players
- **Here Comes Team Rocket!** (#015/071) - Reveals both players' hands
- **Rainbow Energy** (#017/080) - First Special Energy providing any type
- **Dark Raichu** (#083) - Secret rare card, highly sought after
