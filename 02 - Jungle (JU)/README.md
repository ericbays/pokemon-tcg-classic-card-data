# Jungle (JU)

The second Pokemon TCG expansion set, released on June 16, 1999. Jungle introduced 64 new cards featuring Pokemon from the Safari Zone and surrounding jungle areas, including fan favorites like Scyther, Snorlax, and the Eeveelutions.

## Notable Cards

- **Scyther** (#010/026) - Powerful Basic Pokemon with free retreat
- **Wigglytuff** (#016/032) - "Do the Wave" attack dominated competitive play
- **Mr. Mime** (#006/022) - Invisible Wall Pokemon Power blocked big attacks
- **Snorlax** (#011/027) - Thick Skinned Pokemon Power protected from Trainer damage
- **Eevee** (#051) - Key Basic for the popular Eeveelution cards
