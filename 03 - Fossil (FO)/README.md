# Fossil (FO)

The third Pokemon TCG expansion set, released on October 10, 1999. Fossil introduced 62 cards featuring prehistoric Pokemon revived from fossils, the legendary birds, and the powerful Ghost-type evolution line culminating in Gengar.

## Notable Cards

- **Muk** (#013/028) - Toxic Gas Pokemon Power shuts down all other Pokemon Powers
- **Aerodactyl** (#001/016) - Prehistoric Power prevents evolution
- **Gengar** (#005/020) - Curse ability and powerful Psychic attacks
- **Dragonite** (#004/019) - Step In ability provides strategic flexibility
- **Mysterious Fossil** (#062) - Enables evolution into Fossil Pokemon
